At Jostar Dairy we're passionate about bringing fresh wholesome milk straight from our farm to your family's table .Located in the hearts of Nairobi, Chokaa, our dairy has been providing high quality and dairy products sine 2010.

The company has been very succesful.

We believe in nourishing our community with natural ,healthy and delicious dairy products.Our mission is to maintain the highest standards of quality, sustainability and care in every step of the process.

From grass to grace.

What started as a small family farm has grown into a trusted name in the community .Our journey began with a simple idea: to produce milk the way nature intended - fresh, pure and free from unnecessary additives.Over the years, we've combined traditional farming practices with modern technology to practices with modern technology to ensure that every drop of milk meets our customers' expectation.
